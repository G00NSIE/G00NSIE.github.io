Get to Know the Crew

Our Objective

Welcome to Bug House Productions—where the pavement is our canvas and every kickflip is a stroke of art. We’re a crew of high-energy skate enthusiasts who live for the thrill of the ride and the joy of creation. Our days are spent mastering tricks, filming raw skate sessions, and crafting videos that capture the essence of street skating. For us, it’s all about sharing the stoke with the world, one video at a time. Join us on this wild ride, as we explore the urban jungle, seeking adventure and leaving our mark on the skateboarding scene.
